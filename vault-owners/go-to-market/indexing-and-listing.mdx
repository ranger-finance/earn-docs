title: Indexing & Listing on Ranger
description: Get your vault listed on the Ranger UI
---

Vaults are **not automatically listed** on the Ranger UI. You must go through a manual indexing process to make your vault visible to users on [voltr.xyz](https://voltr.xyz).

## How Indexing Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete setup">
    Create your vault and complete all setup (strategies, metadata, allocation)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact the Ranger team">
    Provide your vault details for review
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and indexing">
    The Ranger team reviews and indexes your vault
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Your vault appears on the Ranger UI for users to discover and deposit
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Indexing is a manual process. Plan for some lead time between completing your vault setup and being listed on the Ranger UI.
</Info>

## Requirements for Listing

| Requirement | Description |
|---|---|
| **Vault public key** | The on-chain address of your vault |
| **Vault description** | A clear description of the vault's strategy and risk profile |
| **X (Twitter) link** | Your team/project's X account for verification |
| **Logo** | A high-quality logo image for display on the Ranger UI |
| **Webpage** | A link to your project's website or landing page |
| **Documentation** | Link to any strategy documentation or risk disclosures |

## What Gets Displayed

Once indexed, your vault will appear on the Ranger UI with:

- Vault name and description
- Current APY
- Total value locked (TVL)
- Asset type
- Your team's branding (logo, links)

## Tips for a Smooth Listing

- **Complete all setup first** — don't request listing until strategies are initialized, funds are allocated, and the vault is generating yield
- **Set up metadata** — ensure your LP token has proper metadata ([LP Token Metadata](/vault-owners/initialization/lp-metadata))
- **Verify your LP token** — complete Jupiter verification to avoid wallet warnings ([Token Verification](/vault-owners/go-to-market/token-verification))
- **Have automation running** — the Ranger team may check that your vault is actively managed
- **Prepare marketing materials** — have your description, logo, and links ready
