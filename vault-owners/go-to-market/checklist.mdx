After your vault is created, strategies are initialized, and funds are allocated, you need to make your vault discoverable to users.

## Post-Setup Checklist

- [ ] **Vault created** with correct configuration — [Vault Creation](/vault-owners/initialization/create-vault)
- [ ] **LP token metadata set up** — name, symbol, logo — [LP Token Metadata](/vault-owners/initialization/lp-metadata)
- [ ] **Strategies initialized** and funds allocated — [Strategy Setup Guide](/vault-owners/strategies/setup-guide)
- [ ] **Automation running** — rebalancing, reward claiming — [Running Bots & Scripts](/vault-owners/operations/bots-and-scripts)
- [ ] **LP token verified on Jupiter** to avoid wallet warnings — [Token Verification](/vault-owners/go-to-market/token-verification)
- [ ] **Contact Ranger team** for indexing and listing — [Indexing & Listing](/vault-owners/go-to-market/indexing-and-listing)
